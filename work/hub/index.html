Before & After

The transformation

Before

Disconnected experience. Siloed content sections, no clear user journey, low engagement and poor mobile performance.

After

Cohesive wellness platform. Personalized dashboard, guided journeys, and clear pathways to action.

The Problem

A platform with great content that nobody used.

No clear path forward

  • No personalization — users landed on a generic dashboard.
  • Content buried in category menus, not surfaced contextually.
  • No connection between learning, action steps, and real outcomes.

Engagement metrics told the real story

  • High bounce rates on the main dashboard.
  • Employers couldn't prove ROI on the wellness program.
  • The platform felt like a content library, not a coaching product.

The Solution

Personalization as the foundation.

1
Personalized dashboard
Each user sees content built around their financial situation, not a generic home screen.
2
Guided learning journeys
Content organized into actionable paths, not category buckets.
3
Progress & momentum
Built-in milestones so sessions never ended without direction.

Impact

Shipped fast. Performed.

Learnings

What I took away.

01
Speed builds trust
Shipping a working demo in two months changed how the product team thought about what was possible.
02
Personalization isn't optional
Generic content experiences don't change behavior. Context does.
03
Design systems accelerate everything
Building the component library first paid off in every subsequent sprint.
