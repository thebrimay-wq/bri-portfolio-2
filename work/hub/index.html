Platform Redesign · Financial Finesse

Financial Finesse Hub

Redesigning a financial wellness platform to drive engagement, personalization, and sales impact.

Designed and launched a full platform redesign under tight timelines, helping reposition the product and support new business wins.

Role
Lead Product Designer
Timeline
~4–8 weeks
Company
Financial Finesse
Hero Visual

The Trigger

We lost two major deals to competitors — both citing that our platform felt outdated and the experience lacked modern engagement.

This became a high-priority, company-wide initiative.

The directive was clear: redesign the entire platform — fast — and create something that could compete in live sales conversations immediately.

The Problem

A platform users weren't coming back to.

The existing Hub had evolved into a "kitchen sink" product over several years. The experience felt cluttered, overwhelming, and difficult to navigate.

Outdated Overwhelming Hard to navigate

Users were not returning, and the product wasn't creating momentum toward action.

View solution →

Key Insight

The problem wasn't just visual — it was behavioral.

Users didn't need more financial content. They needed clarity, personalization, and motivation to take the next step.

The experience needed to shift.

Static → Personalized
Overwhelming → Simple
Everything → What matters most right now

My Role

End-to-end ownership.

I led the end-to-end redesign as the sole product designer, partnering closely with the Chief Product Officer and engineering.

  • Product direction & UX strategy
  • Full UI design — every screen and interaction
  • Design system
  • AI experience integration (Aimee)
  • Cross-functional alignment with execs and engineering

This was a highly visible, fast-moving project used directly in sales demos and reviewed by leadership daily.

The Solution

Four pillars of the redesign.

01

Modern, focused experience

Modernized and simplified the entire interface to create clarity and reduce cognitive load. The homepage was redesigned to focus users on what matters most — their next step — instead of presenting everything at once.

Hub Homepage
02

Personalized experience

Shifted the experience from static content to personalized guidance. Introduced tailored recommendations, curated content, and dynamic pathways based on user inputs — making the platform feel relevant and actionable.

Personalization
03

Content designed for engagement

Redesigned the content experience to feel familiar and engaging, inspired by platforms like Netflix. Content is now visually driven, easy to scan, and organized into personalized sections — making exploration intuitive and increasing the likelihood of continued engagement.

  • Visually driven
  • Easy to scan
  • Organized into personalized sections
Content Experience
04

AI-powered guidance

Integrated Aimee AI across the platform to provide real-time, contextual guidance. Built on a closed knowledge system, Aimee delivers safe, personalized, and actionable responses.

  • Understand financial concepts
  • Navigate decisions
  • Take action on next steps
Aimee AI

Impact

Moved the needle where it mattered.

Helped win new business, including the Federal Reserve

Strengthened product positioning in sales conversations

Improved perceived usability and modernity across clients and prospects

Positive feedback from user testing and client demos

The redesign transformed the Hub from an outdated experience into a modern, competitive product.

Constraints

Shipped under extreme pressure.

This project was executed under extreme time pressure with a lean team and continuously expanding scope.

  • ~1 month to design the full platform
  • Continuous scope expansion during development
  • Lean team with high expectations

I worked closely with engineering throughout to ensure fast, high-quality execution.

What's Next

Still evolving.

We're continuing to evolve the experience:

  • Redesigning onboarding for a stronger first-time user experience
  • Making Aimee AI more agentic and proactive
  • Expanding personalization through deeper data integration
