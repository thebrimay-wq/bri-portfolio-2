Creative Director & Lead Product Designer · Financial Finesse

Redesigned a financial wellness platform trusted by Fortune 500 companies — increasing engagement 200% and winning enterprise clients including Meta, the Federal Reserve, and the NFLPA

Context

Financial Finesse serves Fortune 500 companies, government institutions, and major enterprise clients — delivering financial wellness to their employees at scale. The content library was strong, but the product hadn't kept pace. Competitors were winning deals by showing more modern, engaging experiences. The platform felt dated, hard to navigate, and couldn't compete in a live sales demo. Leadership needed a response, fast.

"The product feels outdated and hard to navigate."
Trusted by
Meta NFLPA CVS Health Federal Reserve Patagonia JPMorgan Chase McKinsey & Co. Nestlé Comcast General Mills
Problem
  • Low engagement — users weren't returning after their first session
  • Unclear value — the product didn't communicate what to do next
  • Static experience — no personalization, no dynamic content, no momentum
  • Weak sales demos — the UI couldn't compete visually in live pitches
Before

The existing experience — cluttered, static, and difficult to navigate.

My Role
Creative Director & Lead Product Designer
Set the visual and product direction end to end across the full platform redesign.
UX & Visual System
Designed every screen, interaction, and component from blank canvas to shipped.
Cross-functional Leadership
Partnered directly with the CPO, engineering, and sales throughout the project.
Aimee AI Integration
Drove the design and integration of Aimee — the platform's AI-powered guidance layer.
Key Decisions
01
Focus on immediate value
Redesigned the homepage around a single focused action — the user's most important next step — rather than surfacing everything at once. Reduced cognitive load, increased forward momentum.
02
Introduce AI personalization
Integrated Aimee AI to deliver real-time contextual guidance throughout the platform. Shifted the experience from a static content library to a dynamic, responsive coaching product.
03
Design for sales demos
Built with live sales conversations in mind from day one. Every screen had to communicate value instantly — even to a prospect seeing it for the first time in a meeting room.
04
Modernize the UI system
Created a new design system from scratch — clean components, consistent spacing, and a visual language that felt current, trustworthy, and scalable beyond this launch.
Solution
Focused homepage
Replaced the cluttered dashboard with a clean, action-oriented home screen. Users land on their most relevant next step — not a wall of content.
AI-powered guidance
Aimee AI provides real-time, contextual coaching throughout the platform. Built on a closed knowledge system for safe, accurate, and actionable financial guidance.
Personalized content system
Content is now dynamically surfaced based on the user's financial situation, goals, and history — not a generic category browse. More Netflix, less textbook.
Cohesive design system
A full component library built for speed and consistency — enabling fast iteration without sacrificing visual quality or breaking the product's coherence.
