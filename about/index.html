About

Creative by trade.
Competitive by nature.

I grew up on a soccer field — played D1 on scholarship in college, and still lace up every weekend. Some habits are just too good to quit.

These days, the most important part of my life is my family. I have a son who makes everything better, and I spend as much time as possible with the people who matter most. I'm pretty sure that's where all the good ideas come from, too.

When I'm not designing or chasing a ball, I'm shooting photography — mostly families and newborns — traveling somewhere I've never been, or deep in some hobby that has absolutely nothing to do with a screen. Dirtbikes, wakeboards, a perfectly scored sourdough, and warm-water scuba dives are my version of balance.
